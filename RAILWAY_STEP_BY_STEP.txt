## ğŸ†˜ TROUBLESHOOTING

### If deployment fails:

**Check Logs:**
1. Go to your service
2. Click "Logs"
3. See what's wrong

**Common Issues:**

1. **MongoDB Connection Error**
   - Check MONGODB_URI is correct
   - Verify password
   - Add your IP to MongoDB whitelist

2. **Port Error**
   - Make sure PORT=3000
   - Can't use port 80 or 443

3. **Build Error**
   - Check package.json syntax
   - Verify Dockerfile exists
   - Check dependencies

**Fix:**
- Correct the issue
- Go to service settings
- Click "Redeploy"
